About Me

I'm Shelly Veron — educator, instructional designer, and chronic over-thinker who turned a pile of big ideas into a whole creative ecosystem. I help teachers, districts, and curious kids turn mental clutter into momentum.

Think loud color, thick black outlines, and zero corporate fluff. If it doesn't help a real human learn something, it doesn't make the cut.

Bright Mind Collective

The kid- and community-centered wing of the ecosystem — built for homeschool families, co-ops, and microschools who want creative, technical, and career-ready learning that actually sparks joy.

  • Art Classes

    Open-studio art for every age — painting, printmaking, and joyful mess-making, at home or in a co-op.

  • CS / STEM Cohorts

    Small-group coding and engineering crews where homeschoolers build real, shippable projects together.

  • Graphic Design

    Brand, layout, and digital design skills for young makers with big visual voices.

  • CTE Cert Prep

    Career-and-technical pathway coaching toward industry certifications — a real head start before college.
